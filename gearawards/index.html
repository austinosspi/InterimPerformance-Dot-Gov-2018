Gears of Government Awards

Federal employees play a pivotal role in achieving the President's vision for a modern, effective government.

The Gears of Government Awards recognize individuals and teams across the Federal workforce whose dedication supports exceptional delivery of key outcomes for the American people, specifically:

Mission results

Customer Service

Accountable Stewardship

How are Federal Employees and Teams Recognized?

On a rolling basis agencies and executive councils recognize outstanding individuals and teams that exhibit the values and create success in any of the three outcome areas or the three drivers of change.


Each December, the Executive Office of the President selects exemplary Agency and Council Award recipients to receive the Gears of Government President’s Award.

Each May, the Executive Office of the President hosts a Gears of Government Awards Ceremony to recognize award recipients. Agencies may also hold award ceremonies.

Where Can I Learn More?

Federal employees may contact their agency’s Awards Program Point of Contact to make a nomination recommendation for an Agency Award. To find out who the point of contact is at an agency, please email the Gears of Government Award Support Team.
